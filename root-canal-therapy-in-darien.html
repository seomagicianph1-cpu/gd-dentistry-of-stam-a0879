How long does the root canal process take from start to finish?
Most root canal treatments at our clinic are completed in one or two visits, each lasting about 60 to 90 minutes. After an initial consultation and X-ray, we’ll schedule your procedure, and in many cases, the root canal and temporary filling are finished in a single appointment. If a crown is needed, we’ll schedule a follow-up visit to place it within two weeks. We strive to accommodate Darien patients’ schedules and can often offer early morning or evening appointments.
Are your dentists licensed and experienced in performing root canals?
Yes. All our dentists at GD Dentistry of Stamford are fully licensed in Connecticut and have advanced training in endodontic procedures, including root canal therapy. Our team regularly treats patients from Darien and the surrounding area, and we use the latest techniques and digital imaging to ensure safe, comfortable, and effective treatment.
What is the process for getting a root canal at GD Dentistry of Stamford?
First, you’ll have a consultation and X-ray to assess the affected tooth. If a root canal is needed, we’ll numb the area and carefully remove the infected pulp using specialized tools. The inside of the tooth is then cleaned, disinfected, and sealed with a temporary filling. If a crown is needed for strength and protection, we’ll take impressions and fit a temporary crown, with the permanent one placed at your next visit. Throughout the process, we prioritize your comfort and explain each step clearly.
Do you offer any guarantees or follow-up care after a root canal?
