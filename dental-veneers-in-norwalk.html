Frequently Asked Questions About Dental Veneers

How long does the process of getting veneers take at your clinic?
The veneers process at our Stamford office typically requires two to three visits over a span of two to three weeks. At your first appointment, we’ll discuss your goals, examine your teeth, and take digital impressions. On your second visit, we prepare your teeth and place temporary veneers. Once your custom veneers are crafted—usually within 10-14 days—you’ll come back for the final fitting and bonding. We always work with you to accommodate your schedule, especially for patients commuting from Norwalk.

What’s the process like if I want to get veneers at GD Dentistry of Stamford?
Your veneer journey begins with a comprehensive consultation at our Stamford office, where we discuss your smile goals and assess your oral health.
